About Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, popularly known as APJ Abdul Kalam, was an Indian aerospace scientist and politician. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007 and was widely referred to as the "People's President."
